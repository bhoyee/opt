ABOUT OPTIMAL HEALTH

We provide a planned programme of activities to promote the health and social well being of senior citizens on a day to day basis; and short-stay residential care for the elderly in a friendly and supportive environment.

In an era when children and close relatives are busy with careers and raising their own families, the traditional support system for senior citizens has become dysfunctional, especially in the cities. As a result, senior citizens get very lonely and long for an outlet for social interaction with their peers.

It is often not easy for busy professionals or entrepreneurs to take care of an elderly relative in their own homes as some may require a lot of attention and care.

OPTIMAL CARE HOME helps provide the physical, social and emotional needs of the elderly.
